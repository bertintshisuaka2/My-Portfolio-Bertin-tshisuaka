Portfolio Showcase: Bertin Tshisuaka

This repository contains the source code and assets for the portfolio website of Bertin Tshisuaka, a Software Engineer and Full Stack Web Developer. The website, which can be viewed at https://portfolioshow-syf5lad5.manus.space/, showcases his skills, experience, and a selection of his projects.

About Me

Bertin Tshisuaka is a multidisciplinary engineer with a strong foundation in both hardware and software. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Electro-Mechanical Engineering from ISTA/RDC and a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology (Software Engineering) from the University of Phoenix. His practical experience includes over 10 years as a Maintenance Engineer, which has provided him with a unique perspective on building robust and reliable systems.

To further specialize in software development, Bertin has earned certifications as a Full Stack Web Developer from Georgia Tech and a QA Software Tester from Janbask. He is the founder of Divalaser Software Solution, where he has led the development of numerous applications, demonstrating his ability to architect and build high-quality, scalable software for diverse business needs.
